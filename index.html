Your Safety First

Ride with
confidence

Every Chalo ride is protected by multiple layers of safety technology and 24/7 human support.

🚨
One-tap SOS
Instantly alert emergency contacts and Chalo's safety team with your real-time location — right from the ride screen.
✅
Verified drivers only
Every Chalo driver undergoes background checks, document verification, and an in-person onboarding assessment.
🔗
Live trip sharing
Share a live link of your ride with anyone — they can see the car's location, driver details, and ETA in real time.
📞
Masked phone calls
Call your driver without revealing your personal number. All calls go through our secure anonymized relay system.
⭐
Two-way ratings
Rate your driver and get rated in return. Our community-driven system keeps quality high on both sides of every ride.
🌙
24/7 support team
Real humans available round the clock via chat and phone. Any issue, any time — we're always here to help.
